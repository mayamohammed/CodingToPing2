## 1. Executive Summary & Vision

**Company Name:** Coding To Ping (often stylized as codPING)
**Tagline:** AI Agency + Web Development for Moroccan SMEs.
**Mission:** We help Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in Morocco digitize their sales, their operations, and their customer service.

**The Vision:**
Coding To Ping is not just a traditional web agency. It is a modern tech partner focused on **Digital Transformation** through two main pillars:
1. **Robust Web Development:** Building scalable platforms, from corporate websites to complex e-commerce and internal web apps.
2. **Artificial Intelligence (AI) & Automation:** Integrating smart tools (like chatbots, AI-driven analytics, and workflow automation) to help businesses save time, reduce costs, and operate more efficiently.

The brand aesthetic is "Premium, Modern, and Tech-Forward" — utilizing high-contrast dark modes, glassmorphism, glowing red accents (`#E8000E`), and smooth micro-animations.

## 2. Target Audience

Our primary focus is on **Moroccan SMEs** (Petites et Moyennes Entreprises). These are established businesses that are looking to modernize their operations, improve their online presence, or automate repetitive tasks but lack the internal technical expertise to do so.

## 6. Technical Stack & Platform Architecture

The Coding To Ping website itself is a reflection of our technical capabilities:
*   **Frontend Framework:** React.js (via Vite)
*   **Routing:** React Router DOM (Dynamic routing for Service and Solution detail pages)

*   **Design Language:** High-end dark themes mixed with clean, bright service pages. Recurring use of floating orbs, grid patterns, and geometric SVG icons.
